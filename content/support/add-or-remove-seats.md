# How do I add or remove seats?

From **Billing → Seats**. Adding seats raises your limit **immediately** and prorates the cost
onto your next invoice, so you only pay for the part of the period you actually use them.
Removing seats posts a proration **credit** to your next invoice — first drop the members or
pending invites you no longer need on the Team tab (we won't let you reduce below the seats
currently in use), then lower the count. Everyone already on your team keeps their access either
way.
