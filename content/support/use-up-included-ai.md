# What happens when I use up my included AI for the month?

By default, paid AI **pauses** for the rest of the month and readers automatically fall back
to free models — so you're never charged a surprise overage. It resets on your next monthly
date. If you'd rather keep paid AI running past your allowance, turn on
[pay-as-you-go overflow](/support/overflow-options/).
