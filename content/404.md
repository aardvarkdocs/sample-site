# Page not found

The page you're looking for doesn't exist. It may have moved, or the link that
brought you here is out of date.

Here's where to go next:

- Head back to the [home page](/)
- Browse the [component reference](/components/)
- Start with the [quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart/)

Drop the `404.md` file at the root of your `content/` directory to define a page
like this one — aardvark renders it to `404.html` at the site root, wrapped in
your theme, and most static hosts serve it automatically for any unknown URL.
