# Index OpenAPI descriptions for search

Operation and schema descriptions from your [OpenAPI spec](/components/extras/openapi/) are now part of
the [search](/search/) index, so a reader searching for an endpoint's behavior lands on
the right reference section — not just pages that happen to mention it in prose.
